Preparation of chicken curry

This easy indian chicken curry is great recipe for beginners, perfect for quick and easy meal

Ingrediants

  • 750gm chicken
  • 3 onions
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 Tbsp mustured oil
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp cummin powder
  • 1 Tbsp chat Masala

Preparation

Steps:

  1. wash chicken in a basin and prepare a magnut by adding ingrediants(Lemon,salt, cummin powder, Masala,turmeric powder)
  2. Make the onions into slices as well as tomatos and chilles
  3. Take a dish and add 3 spoons of oil, later add Biryani leaf and lavangam and cardamom
  4. once the above ingrediants are cooked in a oil at certain level then add onion pieces and stir it well until the onion pieces turns into yellow color
  5. Then add Ginger paste in an sufficient amount to the curry after 2min add tomato stir the mixture and close the lid and wait for 5min until the curry boils
  6. Add Masala recipies to the mixture and pour one glass of water along with the chicken pieces in it, mix the ingredients and close the lid and boil it for 10-15 min
